 $X$ is given by :
\begin{align}
  X = -2 \ln(U) \\
\implies    0 \leq X \leq \infty
\end{align}
CDF of  $X$ is defined as \\
\begin{align}
    F_X(x) &= Pr(X \le x) \\
           &= Pr( -2 \ln(U)\le x) \\
           &= Pr(\ln(U) \ge( -x) /2)\\
           &= Pr (U \ge \exp(-x/2))\\
           &= 1 - Pr(U \le exp(-x/2)\\
           &= 1 - exp(-x/2) 
\end{align}

PDF of $X$ : \\
\begin{align}
    f_X(x) & = \frac{d (F_X (x) )}{dx} \\
           & = \frac{1}{2}  exp((-x)/2)
\end{align}

\begin{align}
\implies    f_X(x) =
    \begin{cases}
    \frac{1}{2}  exp((-x)/2) \ \ \ x > 0 \\
    0 \ \ \ otherwise
    \end{cases}
\end{align}
